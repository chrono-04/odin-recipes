Carbonara Recipe

  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Total Time: 25 mins
  • Servings: 2
  • Yield: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces guanciale, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

  • 2 eggs
  • 6 tablespoons grated Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
